# About Health

- contagious
```
英 [kənˈteɪdʒəs] 美 [kənˈteɪdʒəs]
adj. 感染性的；会蔓延的
Scarlet fever is highly contagious.
猩红热的接触传染性很强。
Laughing is contagious.
笑是有感染力的。
This disease is not contagious [infectious].
这病不传染。
```
